Development ProcessHow to get started

1

Discussion

Gather as much information as possible, coverage of the project and what are the limitation of the system

2

Reviews

Making sure that both party, developers and management have the same understanding on the system.

3

Agreement

Making the final agreement, documentation, technologies and timeframes, make a milestones for client to review.

4

Develop

Select what are the technology that can be used to accomplish the project and start working on the system

5

Release

When the system is stable, free of bugs and ready for client to use it is time to release the project for use.

6

Maintenance
